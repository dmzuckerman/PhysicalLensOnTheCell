Free Energy References

  • T.L. Hill, "Free Energy Transduction and Biochemical Cycle Kinetics," (Dover, 2005). Fantastic first chapter - easily worth the price of the book.
  • R. Phillips et al., "Physical Biology of the Cell," (Garland Science, 2009).
  • Any bioenergetics book.
  • For free energy in the conformational sense, see: D.M. Zuckerman, "Statistical Physics of Biomolecules: An Introduction," (CRC Press, 2010).
